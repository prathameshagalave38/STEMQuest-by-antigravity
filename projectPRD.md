# PRD: STEMQuest – Gamified Learning Platform for Rural Students

## 1. Product Vision

To make STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics) exciting, accessible, and fear-free for rural students by transforming learning into an adventure game.

The platform aims to:

* Remove fear of difficult STEM subjects
* Increase curiosity and engagement
* Improve conceptual understanding
* Build confidence through small wins
* Encourage problem-solving and creativity
* Create future engineers, scientists, innovators, and entrepreneurs from rural communities

---

# 2. Problem Statement

### Current Challenges

Rural students face:

* Fear of Mathematics and Science
* Lack of practical exposure
* Limited access to quality teachers
* Learning focused on memorization
* Low confidence in STEM careers
* Poor internet connectivity
* Lack of role models in technology fields

Result:

Students view STEM as difficult, boring, and only for "smart" students.

# 4. Core Product Concept

Students enter a virtual world called:

## STEM Kingdom

They become:

"Young Innovators"

Their mission:

Save villages by solving STEM challenges.

Instead of studying chapters, they complete quests.

Example:

Traditional Learning:
Chapter: Fractions

Gamified Learning:
Village Bakery needs help dividing food fairly.

Student solves fraction problems to save the bakery.

Reward:

* Coins
* XP
* Badges
* New Areas Unlocked
