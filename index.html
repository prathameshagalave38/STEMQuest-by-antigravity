How It Works

Your STEM Adventure
in 4 Simple Steps

From setting up your profile to saving virtual villages — here's what the learning journey looks like.

1
Onboarding

Create Your Innovator Avatar

Choose your STEM role — Scientist, Astronaut, Engineer, Robotics Expert, or Inventor. Customize your look with rural Indian outfits and accessories. Pick your language, class, and let Vigyan guide you.

2
Learning

Complete Story Quests

Instead of reading chapters, save villages! Fix the broken power grid using electricity, divide food fairly with fractions, and neutralize acidic wells with chemistry. Every concept becomes a mission.

3
Practice

Defeat Boss Monsters

At the end of each chapter, face a Boss Battle — the Electricity Dragon, Algebra Monster, or Gravity Giant. Answer rapid-fire questions to deal damage and restore the village. Win = Chapter Complete!

4
Progress

Earn XP, Badges & Rank Up

Every action earns Experience Points. Level up from Curious Cub → STEM Explorer → Innovator → Future Engineer. Share badges on WhatsApp with family and school teachers!
